CrossTalk is a Teamspeak 3 plugin that enhances the general audio experience and provides advanced features for commanders.

Features

PositionalAudio

The "Positional Audio" module enables you to hear clients from the position they are in a supported game.

Stereo Position Spread

Ducking *quack quack*

Global Ducking: Reduce the volume of musicbots when someone talks.
Channel Ducking: Reduce the volume of home or foreign tabs when someone talks on the other one or you receive a whisper.

Radio FX

Walkie Talkie? Walkie Talkie.
The Radio FX module enables you to hear clients as if they'd be talking through a walkie talkie.

Channel Muter

You want to listen only to a few selected people in a crowded channel? Meet the ChannelMuter.
